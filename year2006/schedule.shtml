Schedule

The Contest will take place between 9 am and 6 pm. All contestants and coaches must arrive by 10:30 am for registration.

9 am to 10:30 am: Registration and IBM recruiting
Teams and coaches must arrive by 10:30 am to register. They receive name tags and t-shirts. A representative from IBM will be available to speak with the students.

9 am to 11 am: Practice and breakfast
During this time, the contest rooms will be open. Teams are encouraged to use the machines and get used to the environment. Breakfast will be served. Please refer to the menu. All teams' home directories will be blanked prior to the contest.

11 am to 11:45 am: Contestants' meeting
All teams and coaches will meet with the Site Director. The Site Director will explain the rules, and teams may ask questions.

11 am to 11:45 am: Judges' meeting
Coaches who wish to volunteer as judges will meet with the Head Judge in the judging room.

11:45 am to 12 noon: Contest stations
Teams report to the contest area and judges report to the judging center. Sealed contest questions and necessary contest materials are distributed to the teams.

12 noon (approx): Contest begins
The contest will begin at approximately 12 noon. The contest will last for approximately 5 hours.

12:30 pm to 2:00 pm (approx): Coaches' Meeting
The coaches will meet with the Regional Contest Director to discuss changes to the region.

4:45 pm (approx): Fifteen minute warning

5 pm (approx): Contest ends
All submissions must be received by the end of the contest.

5:30 pm to 6 pm: Awards presentation
The Head Judge announces the results.
