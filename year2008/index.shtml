2008 General Contest Information

  • Hosted by: St. Joseph's College, Patchogue, NY.
    • Directions and campus map.
    • The teams should come into the Business and Technology building, from the Atheletic Center side.
  • Date: Sunday, October 26, 2008
  • Time: 9 am to 6 pm. Approximate schedule:
    • 9 am to 11 am: Registration, practice, breakfast, IBM recruiting
    • 11 am to 12 noon: Contestants' meeting
    • 12 noon to 5 pm: Contest
    • 5:30 pm to 6 pm: Awards presentation
  • Cost: $70 per team (including one coach per school), plus $30 per guest
    • Teams must register using the ACM ICPC web site.
    • Payment details:
      • Make checks payable to "ACM Greater NY Region" and mail to:
            Adam Florence
            5058 Sirron Ct
            Dunwoody, GA 30338-5654
      • Credit card payments may be made through Google Checkout. Email Adam Florence () for instructions.
    • Acknowledgement of received payment will be sent by e-mail, and hard-copy receipts will be provided at the contest.
  • Environment: Like last year, the contest will be run from a bootable CD. You are encouraged to download the contest CD and use it in your training. You want the FC9 image, md5 checksum = 5e7c1aa880ecca1d76f74d023ef0e3ee.
  • Languages: ADA, C/C++ and Java.
