2007 General Contest Information
  • Contest rules
  • Final standings
  • Problems
  • Newspaper articles:
    • New Jersey Star Ledger
    • Cornell Chronicle
  • Hosted by: Kean University, Union, NJ
    • Location: Center for Academic Success (CAS)
    • Directions, parking information, campus map
  • Date: Sunday, November 4, 2007. Note that Daylight Saving Time ends on this day.
  • Time: 9 am to 6 pm. Approximate schedule:
    • 9 am to 11 am: Registration, practice, breakfast, IBM recruiting
    • 11 am to 12 noon: Contestants' meeting
    • 12 noon to 5 pm: Contest
    • 5:30 pm to 6 pm: Awards presentation
  • Cost: $50 per team (including one coach per school), plus $30 per guest
    • Teams must register by October 8 (4 weeks less one day before the contest) using the ACM ICPC web site. Teams that register after this deadline may not be allowed to participate, and may not receive t-shirts if they are allowed to participate.
    • Coaches will be notified by October 10 (approx. 4 weeks before the contest) as to how many of their teams are invited to participate. At this time, the coaches must pay the registration fee for all participating teams and any guests.
    • Payment must be received by October 19 (approx. 2 weeks before the contest).
      • Make checks payable to "ACM Greater NY Region" and mail to:
            Adam Florence
            5058 Sirron Ct
            Dunwoody, GA 30338-5654
      • Credit card payments may be made through Google Checkout. Email Adam Florence () for instructions.
    • Acknowledgement of received payment will be sent by e-mail, and hard-copy receipts will be provided at the contest.
  • Environment: Like last year, the contest will be run from a bootable CD. You are encouraged to download the contest CD and use it in your training. (You want the Fedora Core 7 Distribution image.)
  • Languages: ADA, C/C++ and Java.
